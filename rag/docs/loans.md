# Loan Products

The Instant Mobile Loan offers KES 1,000 to KES 300,000 for 30 days at
a 6 percent facility fee, disbursed to the mobile wallet within one
minute of approval. Repaying early does not reduce the fee.

The Personal Unsecured Loan offers up to KES 3,000,000 over 12 to 60
months at 15.5 percent per annum on a reducing balance, with a 2.5
percent arrangement fee deducted at disbursement.

Late repayment on any loan attracts a penalty of 1 percent of the
overdue amount per month, and the account is reported to the credit
reference bureau after 90 days in arrears.
