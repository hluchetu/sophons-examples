# Branches and Service Points

Branches open 8:30 to 16:00 on weekdays and 8:30 to 12:00 on
Saturdays. Branches inside shopping malls stay open until 19:00
including Sundays.

Luche agents — shops and kiosks displaying the Luche Agent sign —
handle cash deposits, withdrawals up to the agent limit, and account
opening referrals. Agents cannot process reversals or issue cards.

Cash deposits at any branch or agent reflect immediately. Cheque
deposits clear in two business days; foreign currency cheques take up
to 21 days.

Safe deposit boxes are available at main branches in three sizes,
billed annually. Access is during branch hours, with two keys required:
yours and the branch custodian's.

The contact centre operates 24 hours on 0700 000 000 and handles card
freezes, reversal requests within the 48-hour window, and general
enquiries. Disputes and fraud reports are escalated to the disputes
team with a reference number.
