# Mobile App

Registration requires your national ID number, a selfie for identity
verification, and the phone number registered to your account. Approval
is instant for existing customers.

The app daily transaction limit is set per account type and can be
lowered under Settings, then Limits. Raising a limit above the account
default requires a branch visit with ID.

Forgotten app PINs are reset from the login screen: choose Forgot PIN,
answer two security questions, and confirm with the one-time password
sent by SMS. Three failed resets lock the profile for 24 hours.

Biometric login (fingerprint or face) can be enabled after first login.
Biometrics unlock the app but transfers above KES 100,000 always
require the PIN as a second confirmation.

Statements, loan applications, fixed deposits, and airtime purchases
are all available in the app under Services. Cheque book requests
still require a branch visit.
