# Transfer Reversals

Wrong-recipient transfer reversals can be requested within 48 hours of
the transaction. The recipient is notified and has 24 hours to consent;
if the recipient does not respond, the reversal proceeds automatically
for amounts under KES 10,000.

After 48 hours, a reversal is only possible with a fraud report filed
at a branch or through the app, and the case moves to the disputes team.
Card transaction disputes follow a separate process and take up to 45
days to resolve.
