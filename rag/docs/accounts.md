# Account Types

The Everyday account has no monthly fee and a mobile transfer limit of
KES 300,000 per day. The Premier account requires a minimum balance of
KES 100,000, raises the daily transfer limit to KES 1,000,000, and
includes a dedicated relationship manager.

Dormant accounts — no customer-initiated activity for 12 months — are
frozen for outgoing transfers until the owner visits a branch with ID.
