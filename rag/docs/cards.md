# Cards

The Luche debit card is issued instantly at any branch and linked to
your primary account. Contactless payments are enabled by default for
amounts up to KES 3,000; larger amounts require the PIN.

Virtual cards can be created in the app for online shopping. Each
virtual card has its own number and limit, and can be frozen or deleted
without affecting the physical card.

Lost or stolen cards should be frozen immediately in the app under
Cards, then Freeze. A frozen card declines all transactions but keeps
standing orders and direct debits active.

The credit card offers a 45-day interest-free period on purchases.
Interest on carried balances accrues at 3.2 percent per month. Cash
advances attract interest from day one plus a 5 percent advance fee.

International card usage must be enabled in the app before travel
under Cards, then Travel Mode, selecting the destination countries and
travel dates. Travel mode switches off automatically on the return date.
