# Tariff Guide — Fees and Charges

FEE-TRF-011 Mobile transfer, Luche to Luche.
Transfers between Luche accounts on the mobile app are free of charge,
up to the daily limit of the sender's account type.

FEE-TRF-014 Mobile transfer to another bank (PesaLink).
Transfers to other local banks via PesaLink cost KES 45 per
transaction, debited together with the transfer amount.

FEE-TRF-019 International transfer (SWIFT).
Outbound SWIFT transfers cost KES 2,500 flat plus correspondent bank
charges, which vary by destination and currency.

FEE-TRF-022 Standing order setup and execution.
Setting up a standing order is free; each execution costs KES 30 for
Luche-to-Luche and KES 60 to other banks.

FEE-WDR-021 Agent cash withdrawal.
Cash withdrawal at a Luche agent costs KES 110 for amounts up to
KES 35,000. Larger amounts must be withdrawn at a branch.

FEE-WDR-023 ATM withdrawal, Luche ATM.
Withdrawals at Luche ATMs cost KES 30 per transaction regardless of
amount, up to the card's daily limit.

FEE-WDR-025 ATM withdrawal, other bank.
Withdrawals at other banks' ATMs cost KES 130 per transaction and may
attract an additional surcharge set by the ATM owner.

FEE-CRD-041 Debit card replacement.
Replacing a lost, stolen, or damaged debit card costs KES 500. Cards
expired in the normal cycle are replaced free.

FEE-CRD-044 Card usage abroad.
Point-of-sale purchases abroad carry a 3.5 percent currency conversion
margin on the exchange rate. No flat fee applies.

FEE-STM-031 Paper statement request.
Printed statements requested at a branch cost KES 50 per page.
Statements in the app and monthly e-statements are free.

FEE-STM-033 Audit letter or bank confirmation.
Letters confirming balances or facilities for auditors cost KES 1,000
per letter and take two business days.

FEE-SRV-052 Salary processing, employer bulk file.
Employers pay KES 20 per credited employee per run. Credits to Luche
accounts land same day; other banks by next business day.
